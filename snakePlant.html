Snake Plant

₱100

Snake plants thrive in a variety of lighting conditions, from low light to bright indirect sunlight. However, they prefer at least 2 hours of direct sunlight or a minimum of 4 hours of indirect sunlight daily. These plants are drought-tolerant and should be watered sparingly. It's better to underwater than overwater, as they are susceptible to root rot in waterlogged soil. A well-draining potting mix, preferably loamy or sandy, is ideal for snake plants. Regular repotting once a year can help prevent them from becoming root-bound.
