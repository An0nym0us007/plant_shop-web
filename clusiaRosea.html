Clusia Rosea

₱140

Thick, leathery, and dark green, growing in opposite pairs. They are often used to scratch names or messages on, which remain as the leaf grows. Often starts as a hemiepiphyte, growing on other trees before sending down roots to the ground, eventually acting as a strangler.Thrives in full sun or bright, indirect light. Prefers moderate water; allowing the soil to dry between waterings. Needs warm conditions, never dropping below 15°C.
