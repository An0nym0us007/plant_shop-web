Monstera

₱800

Prefers bright, indirect light; direct sunlight can scorch the leaves. Thrives in high humidity and prefers consistently moist, but well-draining soil. Needs a moss pole or trellis to climb, mimicking its natural environment. Toxic to pets and humans if ingested, as the leaves contain calcium oxalate crystals.
