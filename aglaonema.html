Aglaonema

₱200

It has large, ovate, glossy leaves that are often variegated, providing ornamental value. An upright, bushy habit that can reach 1–3 feet tall and wide. Produces small, white/greenish spadix flowers (similar to lilies), often followed by red berries, though flowering is rare indoors. Prefers indirect, dappled sunlight; prefers consistent moisture but is drought-tolerant. Contains calcium oxalate crystals, causing mouth/skin irritation if ingested or handled directly.
